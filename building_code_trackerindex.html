⭐ Notable Items — April 29, 2026
CRITICAL Washington State — 2024 Code Adoption Delayed
The SBCC unanimously voted Jan 23, 2026 to delay final adoption of 2024 WA Building Codes until August 21, 2026, with an effective date of May 3, 2027. Ongoing confusion over energy codes and I-2066 initiative — current 2021 codes remain in place. Public comment period for Group 1 & 2 amendment petitions opens May 6, 2026 — one week away.
📋 ICC · ASHRAE · Energy sbcc.wa.gov ↗ ⚠ Monitor public comment May 6
CRITICAL Oregon — 2025 OSSC/OMSC Mandatory as of April 1, 2026
The 2025 Oregon Structural and Mechanical Specialty Codes became mandatory April 1, 2026 — phase-in period ended. All new commercial permit applications must comply. Separately, Oregon's RMSB voted Feb 18, 2026 to require heat pumps instead of ducted AC in new residential construction as part of the 2026 ORSC energy code update.
📋 ICC (IBC) · ASHRAE 90.1 oregon.gov/bcd ↗
CRITICAL California — 2025 Title 24 Codes Locked Until 2031
2025 CA Building Standards Code (CBC + Title 24) effective Jan 1, 2026. AB 130 locks this cycle — no state or local residential amendments for 6 years through 2031. Key changes: WUI/wildfire code (Chapter 7A repealed), mandatory EV chargers, lithium-ion battery occupancy classification, mass timber expansion, and new NFPA 72 requirements for two-way comm systems.
📋 ICC · NFPA 72 · CA Title 24 dgs.ca.gov/BSC ↗
CRITICAL New York — 2025 Codes in Effect; Fossil Fuel Ban Suspended
2025 NYS Uniform Code + Energy Code (ASHRAE 90.1-2025) effective Dec 31, 2025, no transition period. However, fossil fuel prohibition provisions (19 NYCRR §1240.6) suspended by court order — not enforceable. Next Code Council meeting: Sept 22, 2026. NYC adopted its own 2025 NYCECC effective March 30, 2026.
📋 ICC · ASHRAE 90.1-2025 · NFPA dos.ny.gov/notice-adoption ↗ ⚠ Monitor court case on fossil fuel ban
NOTABLE 2026 NEC Published — State Adoption Cycle Begins
The 2026 National Electrical Code (NFPA 70) has been released. Major changes include: arc flash labeling now required for all non-residential service/feeder equipment (1,000A threshold removed); GFCI protection expanded to 60A outdoor circuits; EV charger receptacles (30A/50A/60A) must be EVSE-listed; dwelling load calc reduced from 3VA to 2VA/sq ft; HVAC GFCI compliance required by Sept 1, 2026. Minnesota already formed a 2026 NEC review committee.
📋 NEC / NFPA 70 nfpa.org — NFPA 70 ↗ MN NEC review committee ↗
NOTABLE Connecticut — 2026 Code Suite in Final Adoption (Based on 2024 IBC)
The 2026 CT State Building Code (IBC, IRC, IFC, NFPA 1 & 101, 2020 NEC) is in final stages of adoption as of April 2026 and expected to take effect mid-2026. CT is a uniform code state — local jurisdictions cannot amend. Codes & Standards Committee meets 2nd Wednesday monthly via Teams.
📋 ICC · NFPA 1 · NFPA 101 · NEC portal.ct.gov/DAS ↗
